Your child may attend The City School in Punjab, Sindh, Balochistan, Khyber-Pakhtunkhwa or Kashmir but our mission will be the same and that is, to encourage regional pride alongside a national consciousness that unifies the diverse areas of Pakistan.

Our philosophy of tolerance and diversity not only enables our students to integrate into their home communities within Pakistan but also prepares them for the many international educational and career opportunities.

The class/grade to which a child is enrolled is based upon the age of the child by 31st of July (for schools where the academic session* commences in August) and 28th of February (for schools where the academic session commences in March/April).

Step 1

Admission is based on merit, availability of seats and sibling reference. Those found suitable are issued admission forms.

Step 2

Once the admission is approved by the principal, the necessary fee challan is issued. Admission will be confirmed on deposit of fee as well as the completion of steps 3 and 4.

Step 3

Fresh admissions need to submit the following documents:

  • Parent’s/guardian’s CNIC copy
  • Birth certificate of the applicant
  • Two 1”x1” sized latest photographs of the student

Step 4

For admission through migration, applicants need to submit the following documents:

  • Parent’s/guardian’s CNIC copy
  • Two 1”x1” sized latest photographs of the student
  • School leaving certificate
  • Last progress report

All academic sessions consists of two terms.
